Robusta

Robusta has a strong, full-bodied taste, with a higher caffeine content.

Liberica

Grown primarily in West Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Philippines, Liberica coffee has a distinctive taste that can be described as fruity and floral.

Excelsa (or Arabica Liberica)

This is a variety of Liberica coffee that grows mainly in Southeast Asia. It has a wider range of flavors, from fruity to floral to woody.

Stenophylla

Grown mainly in West Africa. It has fruit notes and a strong acidity.

Maragogipe

A variety of Arabica, Maragogipe coffee, is known for its large beans. It generally has a mild, mild flavor.

Geisha (or Gesha)

Originally from Ethiopia, Geisha coffee has become famous for its complex, floral and fruity flavors. It is often grown in Central and South America.
